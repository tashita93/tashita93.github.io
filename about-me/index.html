I am Tashita, a creative explorer based in Bangalore, India. With a Master's in Graphic Design from Arts University Bournemouth, I have collaborated with many innovative brands in the last 8 years. My approach is driven by an insatiable curiosity and a love for experimental design concepts. Whether the task is to craft intuitive products or create visuals that move you, I transform imagination into impactful design—always with a sense of joy and adventure. My work is a celebration of the consistent pursuit of design that makes you feel.
